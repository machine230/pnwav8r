How It Works

Up and flying in minutes

No installs, no app stores, no IT department. Works on any phone, tablet, or laptop.

1
Admin adds your email
Club admin invites you. You get a magic-link email — one click and you're in. No passwords to forget.
2
Book the airplane
Open the schedule, pick your time block, confirm. Done in under 30 seconds. Everyone sees it instantly.
3
Fly — then log it
After landing, fill out the quick post-flight form on your phone. Tach, fuel, squawks — saved before you leave the tiedown.
